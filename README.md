## Capstone 3: Analysis with Power BI

## Project Overview
For this project, I analyzed four years of sales data (2022–2025) for EmporiUm, a virtual student bookstore chain that does a blend of in-store and online sales. 
I focused on the **Northeast region** and built a Power BI report to help the regional sales team make better business decisions. I loaded the data from Excel into Power BI, cleaned and transformed it in Power Query, built a data model with table relationships, and created interactive visuals across multiple report pages.

## Report Features
**My report consists of at least two pages, each with a descriptive header, and includes the following four visuals:**

**Page 1 – Sales Trends & Category Performance**
- Line chart showing sales trends over the full period (2022–2025) to highlight
  how sales have changed over time
- Bar chart showing sales performance by category to compare which product
  categories are selling more vs less

**Page 2 – State Sales & Top-Selling Books**
- Donut chart showing relative sales for each state in the Northeast region to
  show each state's share of total sales
- Table listing top-selling books including author names to identify which
  general audience books are performing best (excluding textbooks)
